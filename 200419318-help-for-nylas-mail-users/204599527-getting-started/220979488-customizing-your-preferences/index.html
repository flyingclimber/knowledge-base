Customizing your preferences

Visit Nylas N1 > Preferences to configure your settings and interface.

Within Preferences, you can:

  • Choose general settings
  • Set up notifications
  • Define reading and sending behavior
  • Specify how attachments will be handled
  • Set up or remove email accounts
  • Choose the layout and theme you want to use
  • Choose and customize your keyboard shortcuts
  • Define mail rules
  • Create and edit signatures
  • Create and edit quick reply templates
  • Activate and deactivate plugins
